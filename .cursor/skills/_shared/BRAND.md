# Dinaya Brand Voice

Shared reference for all `dinaya-*` skills. Production: **dinaya.lk** (never dinaya.app in public copy).

## Identity

- **Product:** Sri Lanka booking and growth platform for local service businesses
- **Company:** Ardeno Studio
- **Domain:** dinaya.lk
- **Positioning:** Concierge-led booking + growth — sell a **finished booking page**, not "software"

## Canonical phrases

| Use | Phrase |
|-----|--------|
| One-liner | Bookings, payments, reminders, and growth for Sri Lankan service businesses |
| Hero | Stop the WhatsApp chaos. Get a real booking page in 5 minutes. |
| Pain | Dinaya does not replace WhatsApp; it gives WhatsApp a booking link |
| Outcome | Your booking link answers the basic questions for you |
| Local | Made in Sri Lanka · practical · honest pricing · no commission on bookings |
| Payments | LKR pricing, PayHere-ready checkout |
| Trial | 14-day free trial — no card required |

## Plan-tier story

| Tier | Customer name | Message |
|------|---------------|---------|
| trial | Free trial | Try Starter + Pro features for 14 days |
| starter | Starter | Get bookings online |
| pro | Pro | Manage and grow operations — **main plan** |
| max | **Growth** | Automate growth work with AI |

Never say "max" to customers — always **Growth**.

## CTAs (canonical)

- Primary: **Start free trial**
- Nav: **Get started**
- Hero: **Create your booking page**
- Outreach: **Get free setup** · **DM DINAYA**

## Tone

- Practical, local, outcome-first — not Silicon Valley SaaS
- Business pain in the hook, not feature names
- AI is the **growth layer** after booking trust — not the lead promise
- WhatsApp complement, not replacement

## Banned phrases (flag as P0/P1 in copy review)

| Do NOT say | Why |
|------------|-----|
| "AI platform" (as lead) | Reduces trust |
| "All-in-one SaaS" without concrete problem | Too vague |
| "Cheapest booking tool" | Race to bottom |
| "Guaranteed X% more bookings" | Unless measured proof |
| "AI receptionist is live for everyone" | Voice is managed preview only |
| "Promo code works in app" | Not shipped — manual redemption only |
| dinaya.app | Production is dinaya.lk |
| "Book a product demo" | Pitch finished booking page / free setup |
| "Cal.com clone" / "Fresha clone" | Copy concepts only; preserve Dinaya strengths |

## Objection → response

| They say | We say |
|----------|--------|
| We already use WhatsApp | Dinaya gives WhatsApp a booking link |
| No time to set up | Send us your menu — we set up your page (concierge) |
| Customers prefer calling | Captures after-hours and Instagram bookers |
| No online payments yet | Bookings first; optional PayHere deposits later |

## Compliance notes

- PDPA: collect only what's needed; clear consent for marketing
- Influencer posts: disclose paid partnership / sponsored / affiliate
- Promotions: clear terms, dates, eligible plans
