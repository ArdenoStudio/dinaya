# 03 Content Calendar

Last updated: 2026-06-03, Asia/Colombo.

## What to Post Besides Coming Soon

The coming-soon post should create curiosity. The rest of the content must educate, show the product, and make business owners think, "I need this link before next week."

Content should be practical and local. Avoid abstract SaaS language. Show the booking pain and the relief.

## Content Pillars

1. Pain-point skits
   - Missed DMs.
   - Double-booked staff.
   - Owner replying at midnight.
   - Customer asking "price?" "available?" "location?" repeatedly.
   - No-show after a long WhatsApp conversation.

2. Product demos
   - Booking a service in under 30 seconds.
   - Picking staff and time.
   - Paying a deposit.
   - Owner seeing the booking in dashboard.
   - Publishing a Deal for a quiet time slot.
   - Sharing the booking link/QR.

3. Business education
   - Why deposits reduce no-shows.
   - How to make your Instagram bio convert.
   - What every service menu should include.
   - How to use a booking QR at the front desk.
   - How to fill slow weekdays without discounting everything.

4. Founder/build-in-public
   - "We are building Dinaya for Sri Lankan businesses."
   - Behind the scenes of setting up a pilot.
   - What we learned from business owners this week.
   - Screenshots of product improvements, without exposing private data.

5. Social proof
   - First pilot setup.
   - First booking.
   - Owner quote.
   - Before/after calendar.
   - "This business now has a booking link."

6. Offers
   - Founder 50.
   - Free setup.
   - 60 days Pro for qualified early businesses.
   - Bring 3 businesses, get account credit.

7. Partner/creator content
   - Creator setting up their own booking page.
   - Local business owner walkthrough.
   - "Book my next appointment through Dinaya."

## Production Rules

- Keep videos under 35 seconds unless it is a walkthrough.
- Show the product in the first 3 seconds for demo content.
- Put the business outcome in the hook, not the feature name.
- Use local examples: salon in Colombo, clinic in Kandy, barber in Gampaha.
- Add subtitles. Many people watch without sound.
- Avoid huge AI claims.
- Do not say promo codes are live until they are coded.
- Do not show real customer data unless the customer and business have approved it.
