# 04 Outreach Playbooks

Last updated: 2026-06-03, Asia/Colombo.

## Outreach Strategy

The first launch channel should be founder-led direct outreach. Social content creates legitimacy, but DMs, WhatsApp, calls, and referrals will get the first businesses live faster.

Do not pitch "SaaS." Pitch a finished booking page.

Core offer:

> We will set up your booking page for free. Your customers can book from Instagram, WhatsApp, or a QR code.

## Lead Sources

Build a sheet from:

- Instagram business pages.
- TikTok local service creators.
- Google Maps listings.
- Facebook pages and groups.
- Wedding vendor lists.
- Salon/spa/barber directories.
- Friends/family referrals.
- Local creators who already promote service businesses.
- Existing Ardeno Studio contacts.

## Lead Scoring

Score 1-5 for each.

| Field | High score signal |
| --- | --- |
| Appointment pain | Posts "DM to book", "call to reserve", or manually replies to availability comments. |
| Social activity | Posts weekly and has recent comments/DM-style questions. |
| Service clarity | Has clear services, prices, durations, staff, or branches. |
| No-show risk | Offers high-value appointments, bridal/beauty/clinic/consulting, or peak-time slots. |
| Owner accessibility | Phone/WhatsApp visible or active DM replies. |
| Willingness to try | Already uses links, forms, QR codes, online payment, or Google Business. |

Prioritize leads scoring 20+ out of 30.

## DM Script: Beauty/Wellness

Message 1:

> Hey [Name], saw [Business] on Instagram. Your services look really bookable, but it looks like customers still need to DM/call to find a time. We are launching Dinaya, a Sri Lankan booking link for salons/spas where clients choose service, staff, date, and time from one page. We are setting up early businesses for free. Want me to make a demo booking page for [Business]?

Follow-up after 2 days:

> Quick one: if I set up the first version from your service menu, you can just review it. No payment or commitment. If it helps, you can add the link to your bio/WhatsApp.

Follow-up after 5 days:

> Closing the first Founder Setup spots this week. If online booking is something you want before the next busy weekend, I can still save a spot for [Business].

## Call Script

Open:

> Hi [Name], I am [Your Name] from Dinaya. We are building a Sri Lankan booking platform for service businesses. I will be quick: do your customers currently book through WhatsApp/DM/calls?

If yes:

> That is exactly what we are solving. Dinaya gives you a link where customers choose the service, staff, and time. You still use WhatsApp, but the repetitive back-and-forth reduces. For early businesses we are setting it up free. Can I send you a sample page?

Close:

> Great. Send me your services, prices, staff names, opening hours, and logo. I will set up a first version and send it back for review.

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
| --- | --- |
| We already use WhatsApp. | Perfect. Dinaya gives your WhatsApp a booking link so customers can self-select the basics before messaging. |
| Customers prefer calling. | Some will still call. Dinaya captures the customers who want to book after hours or from Instagram without waiting. |
| I do not have time to set this up. | That is why we are doing founder setup. Send the menu and hours; we create the first version. |
| I do not want online payments. | You can start with bookings only and add deposits later when ready. |
| What if customers book wrong times? | Availability, staff, and services are configured first. The system only shows available slots. |
| Is this only for salons? | No, but salons/wellness businesses are where we are starting because the booking pain is very visible. |
| How much is it? | Early founder businesses get free setup and a trial. After that plans start at LKR 1,990/month, with Pro and Growth for larger needs. |
| Is AI Voice available? | Not generally yet. Core booking is live; AI Voice should be treated as coming soon/managed preview. |

## Setup Intake Message

Send this after a business says yes:

> Amazing. Please send:
>
> 1. Business name
> 2. Logo or best photo
> 3. Services with prices and duration
> 4. Staff names and which services they do
> 5. Opening hours and closed days
> 6. Branch/location details
> 7. WhatsApp/phone
> 8. Instagram/Facebook links
> 9. Deposit or cancellation rules, if any
>
> We will create the first booking page and send it for review.

## CRM Stages

Use these stages in a simple sheet or CRM:

1. Lead added
2. Contacted
3. Replied
4. Interested
5. Intake sent
6. Intake received
7. Booking page built
8. Business reviewed
9. Link shared publicly
10. First booking
11. Paid conversion
12. Testimonial requested

## Daily Outreach Target

For 14 days:

- Add 15 new leads.
- Contact 20 leads.
- Follow up with 10 old leads.
- Book 2 setup calls.
- Complete 1 setup.
- Publish 1 outreach learning or product post.

This is simple, but it compounds.
